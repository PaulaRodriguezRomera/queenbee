Bee Keeping Courses

Courses

Our one-day courses are designed as an introduction for potential new beekeepers or beekeepers with less than 2 years' experience. The morning is a classroom lecture, and the afternoon is a practical demonstration within our hives to allow you "hands on" experience.

☞ Protective clothing will be supplied if required but please bring stout boots or "wellies" and a packed lunch. ☜


Beginners

We will cover all aspects of what you should be looking for at your hive inspection so that you understand the life cycle and learn to read your bees.

Course will run from 10am till 4pm

Price £30.00 per person

Intermediate - Advance

By taking part in an Intermediate or Advance course, you will understand the level of responsibility required to become a good beekeeper. You will get great advice, a mentor and access to training, and a library of books about beekeeping.

Course will run from 10am till 4pm

Price £30.00 per person

Cooking with Honey

We offer a range of one-day cooking classes on a wide variety of topics. You will cook a variety of dishes that you can take home with you at the end of the day. We will be showing you how to cook a lovely selection of recipes combined with honey.

Course will run from 10am till 4pm

Price £30.00 per person
