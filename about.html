About Queen Bee

40 Years of Family Tradition

Queen Bee

We are an independent, family-owned company with 40 years of beekeeping traditions. We specialise in rare, high-quality raw honey. Our beekeeping tradition started with our grandfather Scot over 50 years ago in the Borders. We are committed to keeping his traditional methods while we minimize our impact on the environment.


Our Honey

100% raw and unpasteurized, our Scottish honey is collected and extracted using traditional methods to ensure that the enzymes and proteins are not destroyed during the harvesting, separating, or bottling processes. We aim to promote our beekeeping tradition and passion in Scotland by sharing our love for our beehives.


Our Bees

Our bees live in colonies that contain the queen bee, the worker bee, and the drone. The worker bee and the queen bee are both females, but only the queen bee can reproduce. all drones are male. Worker bees clean the hive, collecting pollen and nectar to feed the colony and they take care of the offspring.


Our Team

Honey, coffee, story, cakes... All these things make our coffee shop great but nothing of this could work without our amazing staff. Our team is the backbone of Queen Bee - and the lovely faces you see when stopping by for a morning coffee or an afternoon treat. That's why we can't wait for you to meet them!
